Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to feel a difference?

+

Most users notice improved nasal airflow within the first 30 seconds of applying the strip. For sleep benefits — like waking up more rested or reduced snoring — users typically notice meaningful improvements within the first 3–5 nights.

Are they safe to use every night long-term?

+

Yes. Breathe Easy strips are drug-free, hypoallergenic, and use a medical-grade adhesive. They work purely mechanically — no chemicals enter your body. There is no tolerance build-up, dependency risk, or long-term side effects. They are safe for daily use indefinitely.

Will they work on my nose shape?

+

Breathe Easy strips are designed to fit virtually all adult nose shapes. They work best when the nasal bridge is relatively flat. If you have very oily skin, we recommend washing your face and allowing skin to dry fully before applying — this maximises adhesion time.

Do they work if I have a deviated septum?

+

Nasal strips work by widening the nasal valve (the outermost part of the nostril), not by correcting internal structural issues. Many people with deviated septums report significant relief, but the extent of improvement depends on whether the nasal valve is a contributing factor to their breathing restriction.

What's the difference between Classic, Sport, and Extra Strength?

+

Classic (clear) is our original formula — ideal for everyday sleep and light activity. Sport (tan) uses a stronger sweat-resistant adhesive and stays on during intense exercise. Extra Strength has a stiffer spring and provides maximum airway opening — best for heavier congestion, strong snoring, or high-altitude use.

Can I use them during sport / working out?

+

Yes — we specifically designed the Sport variant for exercise. The sweat-resistant adhesive is tested to 90+ minutes of intense activity including swimming, contact sports, and high-humidity environments. Over 90% of athletes in our user surveys report the strip staying firmly in place throughout their training sessions.

Will they leave marks or damage my skin?

+

Our adhesive is dermatologist-tested and designed to remove cleanly without trauma to the skin. Slight redness immediately after removal is normal and typically fades within minutes. We do not recommend using nasal strips over active acne or damaged skin.

Are they tested on animals?

+

No. Breathe Easy products are 100% cruelty-free. All safety and efficacy testing is conducted in certified human clinical settings.
