Our Story

Built by Breathers,
for Everyone.

We started Breathe Easy because we believed that the secret weapon used by the world's greatest athletes shouldn't be a secret. Better breathing is for everyone — not just the elite.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

To make better breathing accessible to every person on the planet — athlete, parent, student, or sleeper.

We noticed that the world's best athletes — from Champions League players to Grand Slam tennis stars — had quietly adopted nasal strips as a performance tool. The science was compelling. The results were real. But the products available to everyday consumers were outdated, poorly designed, and overlooked.

So we built Breathe Easy from the ground up. A nasal strip that performs at the elite level, designed for the real world. Drug-free. Clinically-inspired. And backed by a guarantee that puts our money where our mouth is.

Today, over 2 million people sleep better, train harder, and breathe more freely because of a small spring-loaded strip on their nose. That's what we're here for.

What We Stand For

Three Commitments.
No Exceptions.

💊

Always Drug-Free

Breathe Easy works purely through mechanical force — a spring that widens your nasal passage. No chemicals. No steroids. No dependency. Safe for children, pregnant women, and elite athletes subject to anti-doping testing.

🔬

Science First

Every design decision we make is backed by research. The spring tension, the adhesive formula, the strip dimensions — all optimised based on clinical data and continuous feedback from thousands of real users.

🏆

Athlete Tested

We benchmark our products against what the best in the world actually use on match day. If it doesn't hold up at the Champions League, the French Open, or an ultramarathon — it's not good enough for our customers.

How We Got Here

The Breathe Easy Story

💡
The Beginning

A Simple Observation

Watching a Champions League match, our founder noticed half the squad wearing small clear strips on their noses. A quick search revealed nasal strips had been used in elite sport for decades — but the consumer market was dominated by one tired brand with no innovation in years. The opportunity was obvious.

🧪
R&D Phase

Two Years of Formulation

We spent two years testing adhesive formulas, spring tensions, and strip dimensions. We ran studies with recreational athletes, sleep patients, and people with chronic congestion. We iterated until the numbers matched the experience: 38% more nasal airflow, measured.

🚀
Launch

Breathe Easy Goes Live

We launched with a single product and a clear promise: breathe better. The response was immediate. Within three months we had sold our first 100,000 strips. The reviews spoke for themselves — people were actually sleeping better and performing harder.

🌍
Growth

2 Million and Counting

Today Breathe Easy is used in over 40 countries by athletes, parents, healthcare workers, and everyday people who want to breathe better. When FC Barcelona made nasal strips mandatory for their entire squad, the world saw what we always knew: breathing is a performance edge anyone can access.

⭐
Today

Still Day One

We're not done. We're continuing to research, improve, and expand. Better sleep, better performance, better health — through better breathing. That's what gets us out of bed every morning. (And yes, we all wear our strips to bed.)
